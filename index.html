Welcome to the Kingdom of Colchis

About the Kingdom

The Kingdom of Colchis, one of the smallest Kingdom in the world, is located somewhere between Greece and Mongolia. It is seldom visited by tourists as the Kingdom only allows 15 visitors per year to avoid disrupting the ecosystem.
The Kingdom is well known for its mountains. The highest summit is the Meije mount (alt. 1998 meters) believed to be home of the Ancients, the Gods and Goddesses who are keeping watch over the Colchidians.
The Kingdom is also well known for its skilled artisans. It is the birthplace of what we know today as the art of embroidery. Traditionnaly, women embroidered their husband's armors in order to bring them luck and protection. Nowadays, this art is no longer reserved to women and some even say Colchidian learn to embroider before they learn how to walk!
