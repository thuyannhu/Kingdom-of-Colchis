The Kingdom's Royal Assets

In the Kingdom of Colchis, true wealth is not measured by what a person owns but rather how deep a connection a person has with his surrounding. Material possessions are considered superfluous and are limited to what is essential.
This is why the Kingdom's assets are very scarce, as you can see below :
